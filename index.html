Photophonic EP 声光乐影

A collection of sounds, yet picturing a variety of visual scenes.
Enjoy the charm of synaesthetia through this album, Photophonic, brought to you by Polyphoniphile.
Featuring 8 new synthetic ambient music: Kaleidoscope; Stargazer’s Waltz; Cloud Chamber; Aerial view; and 4 others…

*Sparking with Glockenspiel is a bonus track.

Photophonic，之所以叫这个名字，缘于我希望在这个系列里尝试用音乐去描绘一些视觉上的现象与感受。某种程度上，是一种被刻意强调的联觉。
内含8首纯合成音乐。其中，1.Kaleidoscope为序曲，7.Sparkling with strings为尾声，8.Sparking with Glockenspiel为bonus track。
感谢聆听Polyphoniphile的歌曲。
